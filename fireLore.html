1. Powerups and enemies:
    • Homikazee: These enemies will follow you until they crash into you! Their explosion is devestating.
    • This is a Fish... It's a mysterious blob that was brought here during the invasion. It chips away at your health and inables your projectiles.
    • The Air Swimmer is a native enemy, teamed with the invaders. They are equipped with machine guns and a thirst for blood.
    • Don't be fooled by the Boombox's name, when shot it explodes upon impact.
    • The Shotgun will give you the abillity to shoot 3 strong bullets at your enemies. Though they have less range.
    • The Machine Gun gives you rapid fire and slightly extended range, be warned: there's a cool down and it deals less damage.
